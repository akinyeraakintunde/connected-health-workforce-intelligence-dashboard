# Business Requirements

## Project Name

Connected Health Workforce Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective

Provide management with a workforce intelligence solution that supports operational decision-making through workforce analytics, care delivery monitoring, compliance reporting, and resource planning.

## Key Business Questions

1. Do we have sufficient workforce capacity?
2. Which branches have the highest absence rates?
3. Are mandatory trainings compliant?
4. What is the visit completion rate?
5. Which locations require operational intervention?
6. How much overtime is being incurred?
7. What workforce risks exist across branches?

## Success Criteria

- Real-time workforce visibility
- Improved workforce planning
- Reduced operational risk
- Enhanced compliance monitoring
- Better management reporting
